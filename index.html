HABITREEZ

HABITREEZ is a multiplayer habit-building app where your daily habits grow a shared tree. As you and your group members complete habits, your tree flourishes.

⚫Habit Tracking🔁:

➤Users can create their personal habits. (eg-Workout, Reading, etc.)
➤Users can set habit frequency. (eg-Daily, Weekly, etc.)


⚫Tree Growth System🌳:

➤Every completed habit contributes to a growing tree.


⚫Multiplayer Mode🧑‍🤝‍🧑:

➤Users can create groups with friends, family, colleagues, etc.
➤Every individual has a personal growing tree as well as each group shares a growing tree.


⚫Build Streaks🎯:

➤Completing habits daily creates streaks.
➤Maintaining long streaks can help the tree to grow faster and unlock rare features.


⚫Share Moments Of Completion🏆:

➤Users can share photos and clips with friends.


⚫Chat with Friends🤝:

➤Chat system with friends and within groups to cheer and motivate.

Why To Use HABITREEZ?

HABITREEZ helps you to grow your habits with friends, just like a tree grows with care and consistency. It's not just about streaks, it's about sharing your discipline and personal growth in a fun and social way.
